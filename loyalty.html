MarketSquare Loyalty Program

Reward yourself for shopping at MarketSquare! Our loyalty program is designed to thank our valued customers and make healthy eating even more rewarding.

Benefits of Joining

  • Earn points on every purchase:** Earn points for every dollar you spend (or equivalent in your currency).
  • Redeem points for rewards:** Redeem your accumulated points for exciting rewards like discounts on future purchases, free grocery items, and more!
  • Exclusive offers and promotions:** Enjoy exclusive member-only discounts and promotions throughout the year.

Join the Program Today!

Signing up for our loyalty program is quick and easy. (Note: Functionality for online registration is coming soon!)

  • In-Store Registration:** Visit our customer service desk at any MarketSquare location to sign up for the program in person.
  • Online Registration (Coming Soon!): We are developing a convenient online registration form that will allow you to join our program from the comfort of your home. Stay tuned for updates!
